Health Tips

Healthy habits help you live a better and happier life. Follow these simple tips every day.

🥗 Eat Healthy Food

Include fruits, vegetables, and balanced meals in your daily diet.

💧 Drink Water

Drink at least 2–3 liters of water every day.

🏃 Exercise Daily

Walk or exercise for at least 30 minutes every day.

😴 Get Enough Sleep

Sleep for 7–8 hours every night to stay healthy.

🩺 Regular Checkups

Visit your doctor regularly for preventive health checkups.
